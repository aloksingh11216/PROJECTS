Subhas Chandra Bose


  • Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose, the very name evokes a sense of unyielding patriotism and indomitable spirit in the annals of Indian history.

  • Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose date of birth is 23rd of January 1897, Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose birth place was Cuttack.

  • Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose was born to Janakinath Bose and Prabhavati Dutt.

  • Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose was an Indian nationalist in the era of British colonialism in India whose defiant patriotism and immovable nerve and bravery made him a national hero whose praises are still sung with pride by every Indian citizen.
